About Simran Trading Company

Our Story

Founded over two decades ago, Simran Trading Company began with a simple mission: to make authentic Ayurvedic remedies accessible to everyone in Nashik and surrounding areas. What started as a small venture has grown into one of the region's most trusted names in herbal and Ayurvedic products.

Our journey has been marked by unwavering commitment to quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction. We've served over 1000 happy customers, each with their unique wellness journey, and we're proud to have been part of their path to natural healing.

Key Milestones

  • Established in early 2000s
  • 5000+ customers served
  • Expanded product range to 50+ items
  • Became Nashik's trusted Ayurvedic brand
